The Story

4C.
For God.

Fourcgawd brings a distinctive raw energy to the Nak3d Eye roster — a voice that's unapologetically street while carrying a deeper narrative underneath every bar.

Coming up under the Nak3d Eye banner, Fourcgawd is developing a catalog that speaks directly to the streets while maintaining the premium production standard the label is known for. From the Gift From Gawd series to the Best Four You EP, the evolution is undeniable.

The music is available now. The vision is clear. Watch this space.
