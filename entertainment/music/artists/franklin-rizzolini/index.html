The Story

Built Different.
Built To Last.

Franklin Rizzolini is the founder and cornerstone of Nak3d Eye Entertainment. A versatile artist who moves across hip-hop, R&B, and soul with equal authority — his music reflects a lifetime of resilience, strategic thinking, and street professionalism.

From the foundational 30/30 collaborative series with Jersey Demic to the evolution captured in Rizzolini Presents: Toxic Traits and Many Mo' Miles, Rizzolini's catalog documents an artist who refuses to be boxed in.

Every project is a chapter. The story isn't finished.
