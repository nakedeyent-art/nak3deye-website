About Franklin

Franklin Rizzolini is more than an artist; he is a visionary architect of the 973 sound and the founder of Nak3d Eye Enterprises. Born from the grit of New Jersey, his music reflects the raw reality of the streets while maintaining a sophisticated corporate edge.


With a discography that spans from underground anthems to chart-topping visual experiences, Rizzolini continues to redefine what it means to be an independent power player in the modern entertainment landscape.
