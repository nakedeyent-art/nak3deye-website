The Artist

The Voice of
Jersey.

Jersey Demic is more than just a name; it's a representation of the grit and authenticity that New Jersey hip-hop was built on. A frequent collaborator with Franklin Rizzolini and the Nak3d Eye roster, his presence brings a sharp lyrical edge to every track.

With the release of "Salute Jersey," Demic has solidified his place as a premier voice in the state, bridging the gap between the golden era's lyricism and the modern energy of the Southward.

Available now on all platforms through Nak3d Eye Entertainment.
